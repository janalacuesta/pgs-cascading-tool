Did you know?
FNLGHTC is positioning itself to become a Level 3 hospital with 200 beds and 5 accredited training programs by 2028 — and a Neonatal Care Center. We earned the Gold Trailblazer Award in 2024.
What is the PGS?
The Performance Governance System (PGS) is the framework used by Philippine government hospitals to translate strategy into action. It is built around a Strategy Map — a one-page picture of where we are going and how we plan to get there. The PGS is implemented in four stages: Initiation, Compliance, Proficiency, and Institutionalization.
Where are we now?
FNLGHTC is currently at Stage 2 — Compliance. The key outcome of this stage is showing evidence of strategy execution. This means every employee must understand their role and how their work connects to the strategy. Our next milestone is the Gold Trailblazer Award in 2026.

Vision

Far North Luzon General Hospital and Training Center as a premier multispecialty hospital setting the standard of excellence in patient care, training, and research — committed to the holistic wellness of Apayao and nearby towns of Cagayan.

FNLGHTC serves the people of Apayao and Cagayan by delivering quality, people-centered, accessible, and affordable health services; producing competent, compassionate healthcare professionals; and providing policy-driven, bioethical researches that are locally and globally recognized.

The Four Stages

1
Initiation
Planning the strategy
▼
The organization creates a comprehensive plan outlining its goals, strategies, and roadmap. FNLGHTC completed this in 2024 — building the Strategy Map and earning the Gold Trailblazer Award.
2
Compliance We are here
Execution and cascading
▼
The focus is execution and cascading. Everyone must understand their role and how it connects to the strategy. The key outcome is showing evidence of strategy execution. FNLGHTC aims for another Gold Award in 2026.

3
Proficiency
Monitoring and refining
▼
The organization shifts to monitoring, evaluating, and refining the execution of the strategy. Performance data drives decision-making.
4
Institutionalization
Sustaining reforms and delivering results
▼
The highest stage — strategy governance is fully embedded in day-to-day operations, sustaining reforms and consistently delivering results.

Why cascade?

At the Compliance Stage, every unit must show how its daily work contributes to the organizational strategy. Cascading your goals down from the Strategy Map makes your contribution visible — and ensures you are working on the right things.
